about us

Bottom of the Lake is made up of Zacoda Glass (drums), Brett Schlidt (rhythm guitar and vocals), Emeric Meilhon (bass), and Keegan Sweeney (lead guitar).
The EP ‘Everybody Knows Everything About Everyone’ was written between November of 2016 and March of 2017, and was self-recorded the following April. ‘EEE’ was released on May 22, 2017.
‘I’ll Keep This Safe Here’ was produced, mixed, and mastered by Jeff Leber at Prairie State Records in Johnsburg, IL. ‘IKTSH’ came out on July 26, 2018.
